Methodology

How AntiBooking evaluates reports, sources and confidence levels before publishing travel risk information.

What we publish

Tourism-related incidents involving hotels, restaurants, rentals, taxis, airports, attractions or visitor services.
Reports with identifiable location, date, source or supporting evidence.
Cases reviewed by a human moderator before publication.

What we do not publish

Unsupported rumors or vague accusations.
Private addresses, harassment, threats or personal targeting.
General political opinions unrelated to tourism service or traveler safety.

Confidence levels

Each incident is labelled with a confidence signal so travelers can understand the quality of the evidence.

Verified media: reliable outlet or official record
Video evidence: visible source requiring context review
User report approved: checked by moderator
Needs verification: not enough for strong public wording
