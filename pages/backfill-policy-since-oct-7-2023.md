# AntiBooking Backfill Policy

Editorial scope:
AntiBooking should include all relevant travel/tourism incidents from 2023-10-07 onward.

Do not deprioritize older cases only because they are not new.

Instead classify them as:
- Current / recent lead
- Historical backfill
- Resolved / platform action taken
- Needs verification
- Publishable with caution
- Publishable

Eligible categories:
- Hotel / Rental Hospitality
- Restaurant
- Taxi / Transport
- Airport Service
- Museum / Attraction
- Harassment / Atmosphere
- Friendly Places

Older cases can still be valuable for:
- country risk history
- city atmosphere scoring
- platform credibility
- identifying patterns
- AI training data for future moderation
