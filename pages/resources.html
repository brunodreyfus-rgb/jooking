Editorial & Legal Principles

AntiBooking publishes moderated reports based on factual descriptions, supporting evidence and public-interest documentation. It does not publish unsupported rumors, private harassment, personal addresses or calls for intimidation.

Publication rules

No publication without sufficient factual detail
Evidence and source links must be preserved
Businesses may request correction or right of reply
Moderators can remove reports if evidence is insufficient

Moderation-first publishing

AntiBooking does not auto-publish user reports or AI-generated leads. Every case must be verified, sourced and worded carefully before it becomes visible in public search results.

Human review required
